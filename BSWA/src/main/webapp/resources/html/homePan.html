Lookup and quantify gRNA occurrences within your sequencing data

Emera framework provide scientists an easy and powerful way to launch sub sequences lookup on diversified sequencing analyses. Create your own gRNA lookup analyses either by providing your data samples, or by directly connecting to your BaseSpace account and navigating through your Illumina output data.

Analysis processing is performed on the cloud, and leverages todays technologies to take advantage of parrallelized calculations. Virtualized environments, as well as any physical machines may be used to enhance computation capacity, making this framework highly scalable depending on the computations needs.

Choose to export your results either to CSV and/or Adobe PDF file format. Additional comparative analyses on these raw results may be launched in the management tab.

How does it works ?

Input FastQ data
Either choose files from your local storage, or connect directly to your BaseSpace account.
Provide input fastq data file
gRNA Gene library
Provide your lookup gene library as CSV format. Framework will automatically extract gRNA library as well as targeted genes.
Select a gene library
gRNA extraction pattern
Define your own pattern or choose amongst saved pattern list. This pattern will be used to match library subsequences with input illumina data file(s).
Define a pattern
Follow and manage your analysis progress from the management tab. Scale and define your calculations resources from the configuration tab.
Run your analysis!
